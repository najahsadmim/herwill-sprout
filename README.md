**HerWILL Sprout 🌱**

**AI & Technology Education for Every Learner**

HerWILL Sprout is a multilingual learning platform developed by HerWILL to make Artificial Intelligence and technology education accessible, engaging and age-appropriate for young learners.

The platform combines structured learning experiences, interactive activities, progress tracking and an AI-powered educational assistant called **Lumi** to help students develop future-ready skills in technology and innovation.


## About the Project

As emerging technologies continue to shape the future, many students lack access to structured and accessible learning opportunities in AI and technology.

HerWILL Sprout was created to bridge this gap by providing:

- Accessible technology education
- AI literacy development
- Interactive learning experiences
- Multilingual accessibility
- Personalized learning support

Our mission is to empower learners with the knowledge, confidence and skills needed to thrive in an increasingly technology-driven world.


## Key Features

### Learning Platform

A structured environment where students can explore AI and technology topics through guided learning experiences.


### Interactive Activities

Learners can engage with activities, challenges, projects and assessments designed to reinforce understanding and encourage critical thinking.


### Lumi AI Tutor

Lumi is the educational AI assistant integrated into HerWILL Sprout.

Lumi can:

- Explain AI and technology concepts
- Answer educational questions
- Guide learners through the platform
- Recommend learning pathways
- Provide age-appropriate educational support

Lumi is designed specifically for learning and includes safeguards that encourage understanding rather than simply providing answers, preventing attempts of plagiarism. 


### Multilingual Learning

HerWILL Sprout supports:

- English
- Bangla
- French
- Arabic

This allows learners from diverse backgrounds to access educational content in a language they are comfortable with.


### Progress Tracking

Students can monitor:

- Learning progress
- Completed activities
- Course participation
- Achievement milestones

Future versions will include educator dashboards and advanced learning analytics.


### Responsive Design

The platform is designed to function across:

- Desktop devices
- Tablets
- Mobile phones

ensuring accessibility across different learning environments.
